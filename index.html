About

Hi, I'm Or!
A software developer with a passion for building thoughtful, scalable solutions that blend clean architecture with real-world impact.

With experience in full-stack development, backend systems, and cloud infrastructure, I've contributed to projects at companies like ecobee, TD, and Telus. My work spans everything from API integration and data pipelines to AI-driven applications and educational tools.

I recently completed my degree in Computer Science at the University of Guelph, where I also worked as a Teaching Assistant, helping students explore programming, discrete math, and web development. Through this experience, I developed a deep appreciation for mentorship, clear communication, and continuous learning.

Whether it's developing an AI opponent for a chess variant, designing robust process flows, or collaborating on cross-functional teams, I bring curiosity, care, and a strong work ethic to every challenge.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably baking, playing chess, or exploring a new tech idea.
